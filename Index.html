Elgin is a town which is the capital of morayshire situated halfway between Inverness and Aberdeen.
It originated on the river lossie with it's first kirk built in 1140. However It's first official documentation was in 1190.
Elgin is surrounded by distiliries and historical ruins to visit and marvel.
There are many beaches to visit being so close to the coast as well as many shops to visit on the high street which is pictured right.


Seen right is a Map to locate Elgin.
To the north you can find Lossiemouth and the north coast of scotland.
To the east and west you will find Aberdeen and Inverness respectively.
To the south you can find the Cairngorms.


Seen left is the historic Elgin Cathedral, along with this there are many other places to visit which i will go into detal on the visit page.
Elgin has an approx population of 25,000.
Elgin doesn't really have a motto but it's official blazen has "sic itur ad astra" written which is latin for "to the stars"
There are plenty amenities in Elgin such as a leisure centre, bowling alley and cinema.
